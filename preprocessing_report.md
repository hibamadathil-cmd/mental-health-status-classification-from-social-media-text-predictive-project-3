\# Preprocessing Report



\## Project: Mental Health Status Classification from Social Media Text



\## Steps Applied



\### 1. Text Cleaning

\- Removed URLs, mentions, hashtags

\- Removed special characters and punctuation

\- Converted to lowercase



\### 2. Tokenization

\- Split text into individual words/tokens

\- Used NLTK word tokenizer



\### 3. Stop Word Removal

\- Removed common English stop words

\- Used NLTK stopwords corpus



\### 4. Lemmatization

\- Reduced words to base form

\- Example: running → run, better → good



\### 5. Feature Extraction

\- TF-IDF vectorization (unigrams + bigrams)

\- Max features: 10,000

\- BERT tokenization for deep learning model



\## Output

\- cleaned\_dataset.csv — preprocessed text ready for modeling
