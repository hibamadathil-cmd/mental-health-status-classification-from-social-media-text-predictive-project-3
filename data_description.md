\# Dataset Description



\## Project: Mental Health Status Classification from Social Media Text



\## Dataset Overview

\- Source: Reddit mental health communities

\- Format: CSV

\- Task: Multi-class text classification



\## Classes \& Distribution

| Class | Label | Description |

|-------|-------|-------------|

| Stress | 0 | Posts showing signs of stress |

| Depression | 1 | Posts indicating depression |

| Bipolar Disorder | 2 | Posts related to bipolar disorder |

| Personality Disorder | 3 | Posts about personality disorders |

| Anxiety Disorder | 4 | Posts showing anxiety symptoms |



\## Features

\- text — raw social media post

\- label — mental health category (0-4)



\## Preprocessing Applied

\- Lowercasing

\- Punctuation removal

\- Stop word removal

\- Tokenization

\- Lemmatization



\## Train/Test Split

\- Training: 80%

\- Testing: 20%

\- Stratified split to maintain class distribution



\## Files

\- Dataset.zip — raw dataset

\- cleaned\_dataset.csv — preprocessed dataset
